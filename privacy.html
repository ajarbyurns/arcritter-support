Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2025

ARCritter is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

ARCritter uses Firebase to collect basic anonymous usage data, including:

  • App version and device information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or location.

How We Use This Information

We use this data to understand app usage, improve performance, and fix bugs through services like Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google’s Firebase SDK, which may store anonymous data as outlined in Firebase's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

ARCritter does not knowingly collect data from children. If you're under 13, please use the app with adult supervision.
