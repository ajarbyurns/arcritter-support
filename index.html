AR Critter – Support Page

Welcome to the support page for AR Critter, your adorable augmented reality companion app.

This page hosts the official support info for the app via GitHub Pages.

📱 About the App

AR Critter is an iOS app that brings a virtual pet into your real world using augmented reality.
Walk, play, and bond with your critter in your own space — right through your camera.

💬 Common Questions

  • ✅ Make sure your device supports ARKit (iPhone 11 or later)
  • ✅ Allow camera access in your iOS Settings
  • ✅ If the pet doesn’t appear, restart the app and move your device slowly to detect surfaces
